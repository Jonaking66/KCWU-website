Collective Bargaining

We negotiate collective agreements with employers for fair pay, better housing, general amenities, and safety — improving workers' individual and collective welfare.

🏛️

Legal Support

We accompany workers in disciplinary hearings, conciliation at the Ministry of Labour, and at the Employment and Labour Relations Court.

🛡️

Safety Training

We empower, train, and educate workers on their rights and best practices, making every chemical industry workplace safer for all.

Our Mandate

To safeguard all members' rights through negotiations with employers on Collective Bargaining Agreements — securing better terms and conditions of employment, empowering workers, and enhancing good industrial relations.
